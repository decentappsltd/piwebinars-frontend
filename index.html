Welcome to Pi Webinars

What is Pi Webinars?


Pi Webinars is the first DeEd video platform that runs on the Pi Network. Upload and watch educational videos to earn Pi! You can browse our selection of webinars and watch them through the Pi Browser, or upload your own content to start earning.


How to watch a webinar:


  1. Browse our selection and choose one you like. Go to the 'Webinars' page to use the search tools and see them categorised.
  2. Click on the webinar and navigate to the large payment button. Complete the Pi Payment flow.
  3. The webinar will appear in a popup for you to watch and can always be re-watched from your 'Purchases' page in your profile.


How to upload a webinar:


  1. Film your webinar, ensuring you make it high quality and interesting to others. It can be in any language, but must not have any obscene material.
  2. Navigate to the 'Upload' tab in Pi Webinars and fill out all the fields.
  3. Press upload and wait for it to be complete. A progress bar will be displayed- please don't leave the app until complete.
  4. You can now find your webinar in the 'Your Webinars' page in your profile. Pi will automatically be added to your balance when one of your webinars is purchased.


Our mission:


Our world is in the middle of a new economic era propelled by Information Technology (IT) which touches everything in our lives. The learning process is not an exception, with a decentralized education (DeEd) revolution opening fresh possibilities, especially for people historically away to participate in the current obsolete educational model.

Just imagine millions of students from Africa, India, China and the developing world enjoying a learning process about any subject without the high cost and long development timelines inherent to our current education. This is possible thanks to new technologies such as the blockchain where P2P open-source platforms may enter to reach large audiences eager to learn and bring money to the economy. The future of learning is bright with DeEd platforms such as Pi Webinars.
